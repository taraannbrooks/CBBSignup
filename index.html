Book Drive

Date: June 5, 2021
Time: 11:00AM - 5:00PM

The Children’s Book bank fills homes and lives with engaging, culturally diverse books because all children deserve to have books of their own.

When they do, magical things happen - books ignite curiosity, spark the imagination, and open young minds to limitless possibilities.

All donated gently used children’s books are cleaned, restored, and carefully curated by our volunteers and staff. Picture books are thoughtfully bundled to create a home library. Books for school-aged children are distributed each spring to schools at book fairs. In addition, thousands of books find their way into local homes via food pantries, community health care clinics, youth camps, and more.
